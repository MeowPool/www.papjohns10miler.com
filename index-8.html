Privacy Policy

The Papa John's 10 Miler values your right to privacy. We recognize your need for reasonable control over personally identifiable information you may share with us on our website, www.papajohns10miler.com. Personally identifiable information is any information which may be associated with your name. We may ask for this information in order to provide you with product information, goods or services you have requested.

Parts of our site may invite you to subscribe to our mailing list, email newsletters or printed newsletters. We may ask for your email address, telephone number or postal address in order to answer a technical or product question; or to send you technical data or product information. We may use your contact information to send you information about our products and services. This information is not sold, leased or rented to any outside organization. It is used by The Papa John's 10 Miler to support your customer relationship with us. We may use vendors to perform duties such as addressing and mailing. These vendors are only allowed to use contact information to perform the service we have requested and for no other reason.

You may change or remove your name from our mailing lists and subscriptions by emailing us at debbie_laribee@papajohns.com.

We may also collect information which is not personal in nature, such as what pages you visit on our site, what information you search for, and technical information such as IP address and browser type. This data is not associated with any personal information. These anonymous statistics are used to analyze site use, such as which parts of our site are most interesting to our users as a whole.

The Papa John's 10 Miler reserves the right to alter or add to this privacy policy, particularly as we add new services to our site. You may wish to check this page from time to time to see if we have made changes.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, our site practices, or your use of this website, please click here to contact us.
