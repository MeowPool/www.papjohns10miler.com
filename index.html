Welcome to Papa John's 10 Miler. The Papa John's 10 Miler is the final leg of the Louisville Triple Crown of Running. Papa John's has sponsored the 10 Miler for nineteen years. Each year, the enthusiasm and participation grows resulting in record-breaking numbers. The race starts at
8:00 AM on March 23, 2019.
