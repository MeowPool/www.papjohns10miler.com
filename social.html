Get Your Results on Social Media

The Louisville Triple Crown of Running℠ (LTCOR) presented by Novo Nordisk understands that live runner tracking and social media messaging are rapidly becoming the standard in the running industry. Race participants want to connect with their colleagues, friends and family to share their excitement and pride in accomplishing a goal. The LTCOR is proud to be the first running event in the area to provide that service, joining such other elite events as the Disney Marathon, Army 10 Miler and the L.A. Marathon.

Gault Race Management, long-time partner with the LTCOR, is bringing ChronoTrack Live Text and Social Media Messaging to the three-race series. Runners' times are communicated to anyone who signs up for the service. Family and friends are alerted to a runner's finish time for each race, along with the 5-mile split for the 10 Miler. This allows them to share the excitement and cheer their runner at the end. With CTlive, runners times are collected at select timing locations on the course and communicated through four media: Text (SMS), Email, Facebook and Twitter. All messages feature the race name, runner name, time at location, pace and projected finish time.
